About Me 

A music, events and lifestyle photographer from Sydney, Australia currently residing in Bali, Indonesia. With over 6 years experience, I've been fortunate enough to shoot some of Australia's biggest music festivals and music acts! Earlier this year I was invited to join the Utra Music Festival team and was flown to Singapore to shoot some of the world's biggest acts. In addition to shooting music events, I also shoot food, lifestyle and fashion clients.
